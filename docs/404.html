Migovec Survey Data

Survey and 3D file viewers

This site presents data from the exploration of the Migovec System. The cave is located in the Julian Alps of the North West of Slovenia, near to Tolmin. It has been jointly explored by the Jamarska Sekcija - Planinsko dru≈°tvo Tolmin (JSPDT) since the 1970s, joined by the Imperial College Caving Club (ICCC) since 1994.

You can view the 3D survey of the cave, as well as the 2D survey drawings that we produce. The survey data is available in our Github repository and the files presented here will automatically update with the latest data.
